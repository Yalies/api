Remove My Data

Yalies sources its student data from the Yale Face Book and the Yale Directory.

You can use the Yale Face Book interactive website to prevent your information from being publicly available, both on Yale websites like the Face Book and on student-run websites like Yalies.

Click Next to start the process of removing your information.

  1. Go to the Yale Face Book.
  2. Sign in to the Face Book with CAS.
  3. Click Edit on the top of the page.

On the Edit page, you'll see a number of "Yes" and "No" radio buttons, which each correspond to a data point Yale provides. Select the "No" option for any data point that you don't wish to be publicly accessible.

Please remember which data points you have chosen to hide.

Click the Update button to save changes.

Your information has been hidden from the Face Book. To expedite the process of removal from Yalies, choose what you have hidden below.

Please note: while the data deletion will be immediate, if you choose to re-enable a data point in the future, it will not be displayed on Yalies until the next time data is updated from the Face Book, which may take around a week.

If you did not complete the previous steps correctly, your data will reappear in Yalies the next time data is updated from the Face Book.
